Roadmap principles:
- Outcomes not outputs — "Reduce onboarding drop-off by 20%" not "Build onboarding wizard"
- Now / Next / Later — three horizons, not a 12-month Gantt chart
- Revisit quarterly — plans that can't change aren't plans, they're commitments to be wrong

Discovery checklist (before writing a PRD):
- [ ] Talked to at least 3 users who have the problem
- [ ] Quantified the scope of the problem (how many users, how often)
- [ ] Reviewed analytics to understand the current behaviour
- [ ] Checked what competitors do (and don't copy blindly)

Definition of Ready (before sprint):
- [ ] Problem and success metrics defined
- [ ] User stories written
- [ ] Scope explicitly stated (in AND out)
- [ ] Design reviewed by at least one engineer
- [ ] Dependencies identified and unblocked
